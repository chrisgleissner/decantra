1. Google Play Console → **Create app**.
2. App name: **Decantra**

## 2) Store listing copy

### App name
- **Decantra**

### Short description (max 80 chars)
- **A calming bottle-sorting puzzle with elegant, tactile gameplay.**

### Full description
```
Decantra is a calming bottle‑sorting puzzle that rewards clear thinking and satisfying moves.

• Sort liquids by colour across beautifully lit bottles
• Smooth, tactile animations and responsive controls
• Progressive difficulty that stays fair and relaxing
• Quick sessions or longer, thoughtful play

Every level is deterministic and solvable, and every move has meaning. Relax into a clean, modern puzzle experience designed to feel good from the first pour to the final perfect stack.
```
