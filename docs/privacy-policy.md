# Privacy Policy — Decantra

**Effective date:** 1 February 2026

## Overview

Decantra does not collect, store, or share personal data. The app operates locally on your device.

## Data collection

- **Personal data:** Not collected.
- **Analytics:** Not used.
- **Crash reporting:** Not used.
- **Advertising:** Not used.
- **Login/Account:** Not required.

## Network communication

The app does not require network access and does not send data off-device.

## Children’s privacy

The app does not target children and does not collect any personal information.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page.
