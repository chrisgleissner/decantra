Privacy Policy - Decantra

Effective date: 1 February 2026

1. Overview

Decantra is a single-player puzzle game that operates entirely offline on your device.

We do not collect, store, process, or share personal data.

The application is built using the Unity game engine. No third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, attribution, or tracking SDKs are included.

2. Data Collection

Decantra does not collect any personal information.

Specifically:

- Personal data: Not collected
- Device identifiers: Not accessed
- Analytics: Not used
- Crash reporting: Not used
- Advertising: Not used
- Account creation or login: Not required

No data is transmitted to the developer or to any third party.

3. Network Communication

The app does not require internet access and does not initiate outbound network connections during normal gameplay.

All gameplay and storage occur locally on your device.

4. Data Storage

Game progress is stored locally on your device.

If you uninstall the app, locally stored game data may be deleted according to your device settings.

The developer has no access to locally stored data.
